Physical Education & Sports

V.M. Parulekar Higher Secondary School of Arts and Commerce is dedicated in its effort to promote Physical Education and Sports. Through its sports facilities, able Sports Director, support staff, students and their performance, the college displays its seriousness towards sports and physical fitness. Research supports the importance of movement in educating both mind and body. Physical education and sports contribute directly to development of physical competence and fitness. It also helps students to make informed choices and understand the value of leading a physically active lifestyle. A healthy and physically active student is more likely to be academically motivated, alert, and successful. Sports provides an excellent opportunity to enhance ones mental and physical fitness. Hence,V.M. Parulekar Higher Secondary School of Arts and Commerce we at are ardent supporters of sports and the overall development of sports not only on campus but throughout the state of Goa as well.
