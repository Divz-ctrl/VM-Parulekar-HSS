MANAGER/CO-CHAIRMAN'S MESSAGE

Vishwanath Mahadeo Parulekar Higher Secondary School, Verem, Bardez Goa was established in June 1994 under the Chairman, the eminent industrialist, politician and philanthropist, Mr. Suresh V. Parulekar, primarily aimed at imparting higher education to the students of Verem and nearby villages. The aim achieved in the last twenty two years in imparting quality education to the students of the surrounding area, is obviously seen and felt by all from the Board results of our Higher Secondary. In this Institution, right from 1994, our aim is to help students to develop dynamic personality with a positive approach to life. We also encourage them to develop skills to lead a fruitful existence as productive members of the society and arouse in them a deep sensitivity and concern for healthy moral values. We believe that education is apprenticeship of life. It involves the development of mind, body and soul, facilitating young minds to blossom and bloom into worthy citizens and that education is also a life process, task we are fully committed to. We fully hope that our students come out better fitted and better equipped for life as we trudge along the difficult path of this undaunted yet achievable task. Finally, students are admitted in this institution without any discrimination or reference to caste and creed and the rights of conscience are fully respected
